Toolkit Philosophy

The principles that guide the EU AI Act Toolkit.

🎯 Practical

Built on real-world SME workflows. Templates and checklists you can actually use tomorrow.

🧠 Accessible

Written in plain language. No legal jargon or unnecessary complexity. Designed for teams without dedicated compliance staff.

🔍 Evidence-Aware

Rooted in official EU sources and conservative interpretation. Source notes link to regulations and authoritative guidance.

⚖️ Cautious Wording

Uses "readiness," "screening," "documentation support" — not compliance guarantees. Always recommends escalation for high-impact cases.
