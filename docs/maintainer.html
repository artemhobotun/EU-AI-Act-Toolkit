Origin & Purpose

Why I Built This Toolkit

🔍

The Gap I Kept Seeing

The EU AI Act places real obligations on deployers — the organisations that use AI tools, not just those that build them. But most practical guidance is either too theoretical, too legal-dense, or written for large enterprises with compliance teams. Small organisations using AI-enabled SaaS tools every day are left without a clear starting point.

🛠️

The Toolkit's Purpose

This toolkit exists to give teams a structured, practical starting point — not to replace lawyers, but to help organisations understand their situation, document their AI use, and identify the cases that genuinely need specialist input. It is deliberately conservative, well-sourced, and designed for real workflows.

Design Principles

Toolkit Philosophy

The principles that guide how this toolkit is built and maintained.

🎯

Practical First

Templates you can use tomorrow, no legal background needed.

🧠

Accessible Language

Plain-language throughout — no jargon, no complexity for its own sake.

🔍

Evidence-Grounded

Every claim linked to official regulation articles and authoritative sources.

⚖️

Careful Wording

Readiness and screening — not compliance guarantees. Not legal advice.
