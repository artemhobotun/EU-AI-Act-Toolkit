# Official Source Register

This register helps maintainers identify official and authoritative sources to check before updating legal or compliance wording in this toolkit.

This register is a maintenance aid, not a complete legal source list. Users should check current official sources before relying on this toolkit.

## Primary legal text

- Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 - EU AI Act
  - use the official published text and check for corrigenda or updates

## EU institutional guidance

- European Commission AI policy pages
- EU AI Office materials
- Official implementation guidance when available

## Standards and technical guidance

- Harmonised standards and technical specifications may become relevant for practical implementation.
- Maintainers should check current official references before updating templates.

## Related privacy and data protection sources

- GDPR as relevant where AI systems process personal data
- European Data Protection Board materials where relevant
- National data protection authority guidance where relevant

## National implementation and enforcement notes

SMEs should check national competent authority information, local enforcement guidance, and sector-specific requirements.

## How to use this register

- Check the official source before changing legal wording
- Do not overstate obligations
- Prefer "may require review" over categorical claims
- Update [docs/guide/10-source-notes.md](docs/guide/10-source-notes.md) when source assumptions change
- Record updates in [CHANGELOG.md](project/CHANGELOG.md)
