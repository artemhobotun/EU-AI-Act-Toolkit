# Source Notes

This toolkit is based on the EU AI Act and is intentionally simplified for practical SME use.

## Legal Basis

- Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (EU AI Act)

## Keep Checking Official Sources

The EU AI Act is implemented over time and supported by additional guidance, standards, and institutional updates. Always check for updates from official sources such as the European Commission and the EU AI Office.

## How This Toolkit Uses the Sources

- It focuses on practical readiness steps (inventory, screening, governance, documentation).
- It does not attempt to reproduce legal text or provide legal interpretations.
- It is not exhaustive; some obligations may not be covered or may require specialist review.
- Where future dates, thresholds, or classification details matter, do not rely on static text in this repo. Check current official guidance and your actual system documentation.

If you find an error or have an improvement suggestion, please open an issue or PR and include a source link where possible.
