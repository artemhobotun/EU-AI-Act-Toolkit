# Who This Is For

This toolkit targets SMEs and small teams that want a practical, lightweight way to organize EU AI Act readiness work.

Common situations:

- You use third-party AI tools (for example CRM features, support copilots, transcription, genAI, fraud detection).
- You build AI-enabled features for customers.
- You deploy models or model APIs in production.
- You are asked by customers to complete "AI compliance" or "AI governance" questionnaires.

## What This Toolkit Is (and Isn't)

This toolkit is:

- a starting point for inventory, screening, and internal documentation
- a set of templates you can adapt to your context
- a way to reduce chaos when many teams start adopting AI

This toolkit is not:

- a legal opinion
- a guarantee of compliance
- a complete implementation of all EU AI Act obligations
