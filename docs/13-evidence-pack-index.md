# Evidence Pack Index

SMEs can use this as a simple index of documents and evidence to keep for AI readiness work.

## Evidence index

| Evidence item | Template/source | Owner | Review cadence | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| AI system inventory | [templates/ai-system-inventory.csv](../templates/ai-system-inventory.csv) | AI owner / ops | Quarterly | Keep current across pilots and production use cases |
| Risk screening forms | [templates/ai-risk-screening-form.md](../templates/ai-risk-screening-form.md) | AI owner / compliance | Quarterly or on change | Capture first-pass screening and escalation notes |
| Vendor questionnaires | [templates/vendor-ai-questionnaire.md](../templates/vendor-ai-questionnaire.md) | Procurement / IT | Per vendor change | Keep answers from procurement and renewal cycles |
| AI use policy | [templates/ai-use-policy-template.md](../templates/ai-use-policy-template.md) | Ops / policy owner | Quarterly | Keep approved use and prohibited use current |
| AI literacy records | [templates/employee-ai-literacy-record.md](../templates/employee-ai-literacy-record.md) | HR / manager | Annual or on change | Keep training completion records |
| Incident log | [templates/ai-incident-log.md](../templates/ai-incident-log.md) | Security / ops | Monthly | Include near misses and follow-up actions |
| Human review notes | Internal record | Process owner | As needed | Keep the minimum record needed for accountability |
| Source notes | [docs/10-source-notes.md](10-source-notes.md) | Maintainer / reviewer | When guidance changes | Keep conservative and current |
| Decision logs | Internal record | Project owner | As needed | Note why a use case was approved, paused, or escalated |
| Procurement approvals | Internal record | Procurement / manager | Per decision | Keep the approval or exception rationale |
| Evidence pack CSV | [templates/evidence-pack-index.csv](../templates/evidence-pack-index.csv) | Maintainer / ops | Quarterly | Index the evidence pack in a machine-friendly format |
| Maintenance review log | [templates/maintenance-review-log.md](../templates/maintenance-review-log.md) | Maintainer / reviewer | Quarterly | Record source checks and review scope |

## Practical use

1. Create one folder or workspace for each AI system or high-attention use case.
2. Add links to the documents in the table above.
3. Review the index on a regular schedule.
4. Keep it simple enough that a small team will actually maintain it.
