# Employee AI Use Quick Guide

- permitted uses: drafting, summarising, translation, brainstorming, and internal support tasks when approved
- prohibited uses: unsafe data entry, confidential data sharing, and automatic decisions without review
- personal data: handle only if approved and necessary
- confidential data: do not enter unless the tool and policy allow it
- human review: important outputs should be checked by a person
- fact-checking: treat AI output as a starting point, not as a final answer
- record keeping: keep notes where needed
- escalation: send sensitive or unclear cases to the owner
