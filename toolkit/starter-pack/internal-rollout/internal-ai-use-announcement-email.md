# Internal AI Use Announcement Email

**Subject:** New internal AI use rules and AI tool inventory

Hello team,

We are introducing a simple AI inventory and internal AI use rules so we can use AI tools in a more organized and responsible way.

Please do the following:

- use the approved AI tools and internal rules
- do not enter confidential or personal data unless approved
- keep human review in place for important outputs
- report incidents, mistakes, or concerns
- follow the stored templates and guidance

The templates are stored in the internal AI governance folder.

If you are not sure whether a use case is allowed, escalate it to the AI owner or management sponsor.
