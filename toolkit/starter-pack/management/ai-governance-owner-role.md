# AI Governance Owner Role

Lightweight SME roles:

- **AI owner** - keeps the inventory and readiness materials current.
- **System owner** - understands a specific tool or workflow.
- **Vendor owner** - handles procurement and vendor follow-up.
- **Privacy/security reviewer** - checks data handling and security questions.
- **Management sponsor** - signs off on priorities and escalation.

Responsibilities should stay simple, documented, and practical.
