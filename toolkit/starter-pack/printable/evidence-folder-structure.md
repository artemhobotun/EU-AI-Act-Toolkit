# Evidence Folder Structure

Recommended internal folder structure:

```text
AI-governance/
  01-inventory/
  02-risk-screening/
  03-vendors/
  04-policies/
  05-literacy/
  06-incidents/
  07-maintenance-reviews/
  08-source-notes/
```

What to store:

- `01-inventory/` - AI tool inventory and owner details
- `02-risk-screening/` - screening forms and escalation notes
- `03-vendors/` - vendor questionnaires, answers, and procurement notes
- `04-policies/` - internal AI use policy and related instructions
- `05-literacy/` - training records and attendance notes
- `06-incidents/` - incident log and follow-up actions
- `07-maintenance-reviews/` - review logs and maintenance notes
- `08-source-notes/` - source notes and update references
