# EU AI Act SME Starter Pack

This starter pack is a lightweight entry point for SMEs that want to get organized before deeper EU AI Act readiness work.

## What this pack includes

| File | Purpose | Recommended owner |
|---|---|---|
| [30-minute readiness self-assessment](printable/docs/guide/30-minute-readiness-self-assessment.md) | Quick self-check for the most important readiness questions | Founder, operator, or AI owner |
| [One-page executive checklist](printable/one-page-executive-checklist.md) | Simple management checklist for the first review | Founder, management sponsor |
| [AI tool inventory starter](printable/ai-tool-inventory-starter.md) | Basic register for AI tools and features | AI owner or operations lead |
| [Basic risk screening starter](printable/basic-risk-screening-starter.md) | First-pass screening for people, data, and escalation | AI owner or privacy reviewer |
| [Vendor review starter](printable/vendor-review-starter.md) | Starter question set for AI vendors | Procurement or vendor owner |
| [Evidence folder structure](printable/evidence-folder-structure.md) | Suggested internal folder layout for records | AI owner or operations lead |
| [Internal rollout email](internal-rollout/internal-ai-use-announcement-email.md) | Announcement template for staff | Management sponsor |
| [Employee AI use quick guide](internal-rollout/employee-ai-use-quick-guide.md) | Short internal rules summary | AI owner |
| [AI literacy session outline](internal-rollout/ai-literacy-session-outline.md) | 30-minute team briefing outline | AI owner or trainer |
| [Management briefing template](management/management-briefing-template.md) | Briefing note for founders and management | Management sponsor |
| [AI governance owner role](management/ai-governance-owner-role.md) | Lightweight SME ownership model | Management sponsor |

| [Starter pack index](starter-pack-index.csv) | Simple index of the starter pack documents | AI owner or operations lead |

## How to use it

1. Read the disclaimer.
2. Complete the 30-minute self-assessment.
3. Create an AI tool inventory.
4. Screen the most important AI use cases.
5. Review vendors.
6. Set internal use rules.
7. Store completed documents internally.
8. Escalate sensitive or high-impact use cases.

## What this pack is not

- not legal advice
- not a compliance guarantee
- not a substitute for legal or technical review
- not a full risk management system
