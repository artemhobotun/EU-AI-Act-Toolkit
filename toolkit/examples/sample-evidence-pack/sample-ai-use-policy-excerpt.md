# Sample AI Use Policy Excerpt

This is fictional sample content only.

## Permitted uses

- internal drafting
- summarising
- translation support
- first-pass research

## Prohibited uses

- entering confidential data into unapproved tools
- automatic customer-facing sending without review
- using AI output as final legal or HR advice

## Confidential data

Do not enter confidential business information, secrets, or customer data into public tools unless specifically approved.

## Personal data

Use personal data only where approved and necessary. If the use is sensitive, it should be escalated.

## Human review

Human review is required before external publication or important business action.

## Incident reporting

Report unsafe output, data leakage, or policy violations through the internal incident process.
