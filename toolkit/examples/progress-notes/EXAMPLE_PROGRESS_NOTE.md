# Example toolkit progress note

This note shows a lightweight format for recording a small practical improvement while using the EU AI Act SME Toolkit.

## SME use case

A small business should keep a short record of AI tools, owners, purposes, data categories, and review dates.

## Practical reminder

- Identify the AI tool or workflow.
- Record the business owner.
- Check whether personal data is involved.
- Check whether the tool affects decisions about people.
- Keep human review for important outputs.
- Review vendor documentation before procurement.
- Keep the inventory updated over time.

## Compliance note

This is a practical readiness note and not legal advice.
