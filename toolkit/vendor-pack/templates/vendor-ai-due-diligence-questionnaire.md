# Vendor AI Due Diligence Questionnaire

Use this questionnaire to get practical vendor answers before you buy or renew an AI-enabled SaaS tool.

## Vendor and product overview

- Product name:
- Vendor legal entity:
- AI-enabled features:
- Intended customer use cases:
- Deployment model:
- Subprocessors / third-party model providers:

Questions:

- What AI functionality is included?
- Is the product using general-purpose AI models?
- Which features generate, classify, rank, score, recommend, or automate outputs?
- Are outputs used for decisions about people?
- Can customers disable or configure AI features?

## Data use

Questions:

- What data is processed?
- Are customer data or customer inputs used for model training?
- Are customer outputs stored?
- Are logs retained?
- Where is data stored?
- What retention periods apply?
- Can customers opt out of training or analytics?

## Documentation

Request:

- product documentation
- AI feature description
- model or system cards if available
- risk management documentation if available
- transparency information
- security documentation
- data processing agreement
- subprocessors list
- audit log or export options

## Security and privacy

Questions:

- What access controls exist?
- Is encryption used in transit and at rest?
- What audit logs are available?
- How are incidents handled?
- Is a DPA available?
- What GDPR support exists?
- How can data be deleted or exported?
- What admin controls are available?

## Human oversight and controls

Questions:

- Can humans review outputs before use?
- Can outputs be challenged, corrected, or overridden?
- Are confidence scores or explanations available?
- Are risky actions blocked by default?
- Can AI features be limited by user role?

## EU AI Act readiness support

Questions:

- What information can the vendor provide to support customer readiness work?
- Does the vendor provide documentation about intended use, limitations, monitoring, transparency, or human oversight?
- Does the vendor explain customer responsibilities?
