# Vendor Contract Review Notes

These notes help prepare questions for qualified review. They are not legal advice.

## Areas to flag

- Data use
- Training on customer data
- Subprocessors
- Audit rights
- Security commitments
- Incident notification
- Data deletion and export
- Liability limitations
- Customer responsibilities
- AI feature limitations
- Documentation commitments

## Notes

- What does the contract say about customer data and model training?
- Are there clear subprocessors and update commitments?
- Can audit logs be reviewed or exported?
- Are deletion and export rights practical?
- Are incident notification timelines clear?
- Are AI limitations described in a way the customer can rely on operationally?
