# Vendor Assessment Pack

A practical vendor review pack for SMEs evaluating AI-enabled SaaS tools.

This pack is for readiness and documentation support only. It is not legal advice and does not guarantee compliance.

## What this pack is

Use this pack before buying, renewing, or expanding an AI-enabled SaaS tool. It helps small teams ask better questions, compare vendors, record evidence, and escalate higher-attention cases.

## Who should use it

- founders and operators
- procurement and vendor owners
- privacy, security, and compliance reviewers
- consultants supporting SME vendor review work

## Suggested review workflow

1. Define the business purpose.
2. Identify AI-enabled features.
3. Complete the vendor questionnaire.
4. Review data use, logs, and training settings.
5. Screen the use case for people impact and sensitivity.
6. Request missing documents.
7. Compare vendors in the matrix.
8. Record the decision.
9. Escalate legal, privacy, or security concerns when needed.

## What to ask vendors

- What AI functionality is included?
- What data is processed?
- Is customer data used for training or analytics?
- What logs and admin controls exist?
- What security documentation is available?
- What human oversight is supported?
- What documentation supports customer readiness work?

## What to document internally

- business purpose
- vendor answers
- documents reviewed
- decision status
- mitigations and open questions
- review date and owner
- escalation notes

## Red flags

- unclear AI functionality
- unclear data training use
- missing logs or admin controls
- no DPA where personal data is involved
- no useful documentation
- no human oversight support
- vendor refuses to answer reasonable questions

## When to escalate

Escalate when the tool affects people, processes personal data, uses sensitive data, or lacks clear vendor documentation. Do not rely on this toolkit alone for high-attention cases.

## What this pack is not

- not legal advice
- not a compliance guarantee
- not a substitute for qualified legal, privacy, or security review
- not a hosted compliance service
