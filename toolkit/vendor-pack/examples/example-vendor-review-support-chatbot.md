# Example vendor review: support chatbot

This is a fictional sample only.

## Business purpose

ExampleSupportBot is used for website customer support and ticket triage.

## Data involved

- chat transcripts
- email addresses
- order references
- support notes

## People affected

Customers who contact support and staff who review escalations.

## Vendor answers

- AI feature: response drafting and triage
- customer data used for training: opt-out available
- logs available: yes
- human oversight: yes, with escalation to support staff
- documentation available: product guide, privacy notice, security summary

## Open questions

- How are chat logs retained?
- What is the escalation path to a human agent?
- Can the tool be limited from making commitments to customers?

## Risk attention level

Low to medium attention.

## Escalation note

Customer-facing use should be reviewed for transparency, logging, and human escalation.
