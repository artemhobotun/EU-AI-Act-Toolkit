# Sector Packs

These sector packs provide practical, SME-friendly starting points for common AI use cases. They are not legal advice and do not replace a full legal or technical review.

Use them to decide:

- whether the use case is routine, sensitive, or escalated
- what documents to collect
- what questions to ask before rollout
- what human review is needed

## Sector packs

| Sector pack | Typical use cases | Main risk questions | Files to use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Internal productivity and generative AI | Drafting, summarising, translation, internal research | Confidential data, human review, approved tools | [internal-productivity-and-genai.md](internal-productivity-and-genai.md) |
| Customer support chatbots | FAQ bots, ticket triage, support reply drafting | Customer data, transparency, escalation, logs | [customer-support-chatbots.md](customer-support-chatbots.md) |
| HR and recruitment | CV screening, candidate ranking, interview support | Employment impact, bias, human review | [hr-and-recruitment.md](hr-and-recruitment.md) |
| Marketing and sales | Content generation, ad copy, lead scoring, CRM scoring | People-affecting scoring, personal data, human review | [marketing-and-sales.md](marketing-and-sales.md) |
| Vendor procurement and SaaS | Buying AI-enabled tools, vendor review, contract checks | Training on customer inputs, logs, security, transparency | [vendor-procurement-and-saas.md](vendor-procurement-and-saas.md) |
| Legal and document review | Summaries, clause extraction, research assistance | Confidentiality, legal review, accuracy, human oversight | [legal-and-document-review.md](legal-and-document-review.md) |
