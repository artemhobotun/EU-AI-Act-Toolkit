# Sector pack: Legal and document review

## Typical SME use cases

- document summarisation
- contract review support
- clause extraction
- research assistance

## Common tools

- AI document assistants
- contract review add-ons
- research copilots

## Typical data involved

- contracts
- policies
- confidential business documents
- potentially personal data

## Key readiness questions

- Is the tool limited to draft and summary support?
- Does a qualified human review the output?
- Can documents be retained or used for training?
- Are confidentiality settings available?
- Is the output being treated as legal advice?

## Basic risk screening notes

Human legal review is required for legal conclusions; this toolkit does not provide legal advice.

## Documentation to keep

- AI inventory entry
- screening form
- vendor questionnaire
- confidentiality and use rules
- human review notes
- incident log

## Internal policy controls

- no legal conclusions without human review
- no unapproved confidential document uploads
- access control for sensitive files
- escalation route for legal questions

## Human review expectations

Human review should happen before any legal or contractual decision is made.

## When to escalate

- the tool is used for legal conclusions or advice
- confidential documents are processed outside approved tools
- the vendor cannot explain retention, training, or logs
- the output is used in a high-impact decision
