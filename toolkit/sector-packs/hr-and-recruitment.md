# Sector pack: HR and recruitment

## Typical SME use cases

- CV screening
- candidate ranking
- interview support
- employee monitoring
- performance support

## Common tools

- recruiting platforms with AI ranking
- AI-assisted interview notes
- HR software with screening features

## Typical data involved

- CVs
- cover letters
- interview notes
- employee records
- personal data

## Key readiness questions

- Does the tool influence hiring or worker management decisions?
- Is human review mandatory?
- Can staff challenge the output?
- Does the vendor explain scoring or ranking logic?
- Are logs and review records available?

## Basic risk screening notes

Employment-related AI use may require deeper review and should be escalated. Do not rely on this toolkit alone.

## Documentation to keep

- AI inventory entry
- screening form
- decision log
- vendor questionnaire
- human review notes
- incident log

## Internal policy controls

- approved HR tools only
- human review before action
- no automatic rejection or termination based on AI alone
- escalation route for sensitive use cases

## Human review expectations

Human review should be mandatory before any hiring, promotion, discipline, or termination-related action is taken.

## When to escalate

- the tool affects access to work or employment outcomes
- the vendor cannot explain the scoring, logs, or oversight controls
- bias, fairness, or transparency concerns exist
- personal data is used in a way that raises privacy or labour-law concerns
